Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, chives, or dill
  • 1 tablespoon grated cheese (such as cheddar, mozzarella, or feta
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:

    In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

  2. Melt the butter:

    In a non-stick pan, melt the butter over medium heat.

  3. Cook the eggs:

    Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook for 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the filling:

    Add your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats to one half of the omelette.

  5. Flip the omelette:

    Use a spatula to carefully flip the other half.

  6. Cook the other side:

    Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the side is cooked.

  7. Slide onto a plate:

    Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.

  8. Enjoy:

    Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
